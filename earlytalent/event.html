Social Care Workforce Retention Roundtable – Midlands


Join Cohesion & Care Character for our next roundtable event in the Midlands.

This exclusive event will bring together a select group of sector colleagues to explore the key challenges and opportunities in retaining our workforce.

Attendees will have an early preview of Cohesion's 2025 sector retention whitepaper analysing thousands of exit and retention interviews. Insights from the report will inform discussions on the day, where we'll explore what is working for your organisation and others in social care, as well as identifying key areas for focus.

Through open discussion and shared learning, we aim to uncover practical strategies that will strengthen workforce retention.

Tue 1 Apr 2025

2pm – 4pm | In-person
