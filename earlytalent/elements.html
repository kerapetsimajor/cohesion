We become your Recruitment & Retention team

Whether you require targeted campaign-based talent acquisition or seek an exclusive recruitment partner, we are your trusted source for end-to-end social care recruitment expertise and insights.

Our solution is a refreshing alternative to traditional recruitment agency models when you need to get recruitment and retention right.

We build dedicated and bespoke recruitment processes using the best available technology, data, attraction, assessment, onboarding and reporting methods. Let us help you to reduce agency spend and improve retention as we have done for care providers for many years.

Born from a Cohesion sponsored PhD “Recruitment in the UK Care Sector”, we worked with Professor Steve Woods to create Care Character.

The tool is the definitive values based recruitment and retention tool which includes a validated assessment.
