Autograph Care - Outsourced Recruitment

Transforming Recruitment: Autograph Care and Cohesion’s Partnership for
Better Hiring Outcomes. Get connected to the best talent

Contact us

The Client

Autograph Care stands at the forefront of delivering exceptional residential
and dementia care across the North West, Yorkshire and East Midlands.

The Challenge & Solution

Autograph Care faced several recruitment challenges, including high non-attendance at interviews, unsuitable applicants, lengthy onboarding times, and excessive reliance on agency staff.

Autograph partnered with Cohesion to deliver a streamlined and compliant end-to-end recruitment service – overseeing attraction, candidate assessment, compliance checks and retention interviews.

Through a structured and values-driven approach, Cohesion successfully transformed Autograph Care’s recruitment process. By improving candidate quality, streamlining onboarding, and reducing agency dependence, Autograph Care now benefits from a more efficient, engaged, and long-term workforce.

Home Manager Feedback

“All candidates turned up for interview, and they were all lovely. I asked them about their experience with the Autograph Recruitment Team, and they all said it was very professional, and the team was really helpful. Thank you for your support!”

“You are really sending us some fantastic candidates – thank you!”

Results

The partnership has produced significant results, including:

  • 6,472 hours offered resulting in 200 job offers
  • 84% interview attendance reducing no show activity
  • 76% of interviewed candidates suitable for an offer
  • 92% of candidates accepted their job offer
  • 79% of offered candidates progressed to their start date
  • Reduced onboarding time to 24 days from offer to start
  • Reduced time to hire to 30 days from application to start
  • Agency reduction of 86% since partnership began
  • Valuable feedback obtained through 2 & 8-week check-ins
